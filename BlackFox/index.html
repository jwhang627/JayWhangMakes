BLACK FOX

On October 2020, I challenged myself to make a short animated film under a month with a budget of less than $300. I made a fox logo with Krita and the idea came to me. This is the result.

Oh, I have merchandises for this short. They are all all available on my RedBubble page (https://www.redbubble.com/people/Jay-Whang-Arts/shop).

Cast: Michelangelo Slater (https://twitter.com/MichelangeloVO) as Felix Trout Abbey Veffer (https://twitter.com/VultureVocals) as Devil Wings NiKo Vargas (https://twitter.com/snazzledazzle) as Human Head Red Aller (https://twitter.com/chardevoire) as Kindergarten teacher Darcy Macguire (https://twitter.com/DarcyMaguireVA) as Felix Trout, Junior

Featuring musics by Patricia Taxxon (https://patriciataxxon.bandcamp.com/) and Abstractions (https://twitter.com/ben_burnes).
