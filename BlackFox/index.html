BLACK FOX

On October 2020, I challenged myself to make a short animated film under a month with a budget of less than $300. I made a fox logo with Krita and the idea came to me. This is the result.

Oh, I have merchandises for this short. They are all all available on my RedBubble page.


Cast:

Michelangelo Slater as Felix Trout

Abbey Veffer as Devil Wings

NiKo Vargas as Human Head

Red Aller as Kindergarten teacher

Darcy Macguire as Felix Trout, Junior


Featuring musics by Patricia Taxxon and Abstractions.
