March for Queer & Trans Youth Autonomy

On March 31st, 2023, I attended the political march that was held at the Union Square Park. I live a few blocks away from that location. During the beginning of the rally, the right-wing figure by the name of Alex Stein interrupted the speaker and only to get kicked out by the attendes. We all marched to the Washing Square Park. By the end, the organizers held an open-mic session where people share their thoughts and ideas about the better worlds for queer people in the United States of America.
