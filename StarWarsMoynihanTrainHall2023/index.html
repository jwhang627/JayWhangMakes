Star Wars Moynihan Train Hall 2023

I was invited to the meet-up of cosplayers and photographers at the Moynihan Train Hall during the night of October 14th 2023. It was the third day of New York Comic-Con. I asked Star Wars cosplayers to have lightsaber battle and took several pictures of them.

I edited and color graded with Adobe Lightroom. This one was fun to color grade. I actually had an euphoric feeling while doing it.

This pictures feature Matteo Adams (@twiloki_of_asgard), Shane M. (@shanem201), and @ohno_its_mambo.
