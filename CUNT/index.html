Charlie Uniform November Tango

Charlie Uniform November Tango is the segment for Hellavision Television's animation anthology CUNT-A-VISION, which centers around the theme of being "cunt," and the prompt was proposed by the animator Maddie Brewer. I used cut outs from public domain comic books. I made this with Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, and Premiere Pro.


Cast:

Shakyra Dunn as Charlie Uniform, Charlie's mother, Bellona the goddess, & War Baby

W. Blake Kimber as Dan Smith the wolf

Anthony Rodriguez as Charlie's boyfriend

Kat Loveland as Phone Voice
